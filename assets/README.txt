Drop your image files in this folder with these exact names:

  photo-now.png     your current photo (the black & white portrait)
                    -> shows in the hero photo card and the about section

  photo-child.png   your childhood photo
                    -> shows when someone hovers the hero photo,
                       and becomes the mouse cursor across the site

  logo.png          your logo (white on transparent is fine,
                    the site flips it to black automatically)
                    -> shows top left in the nav on every page

PNG or JPG both work. If you use .jpg, rename to .png anyway or tell
Claude to update the paths. The site works fine while any of these
are missing: it falls back to the placeholder mark automatically.
