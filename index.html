Three years of designing things that ship.

I was the only designer at Tejis.ai, an AI startup building products for Indian law enforcement. Before that I worked at TCS on e-learning products, inside a large delivery team with review cycles and enterprise clients. That work belongs to TCS, so it cannot be shown here, but it is where I learned to take feedback and ship on someone else's deadline. On the side, I designed a medical records app that helped a hospital win a national quality accreditation. I am based in Kerala, India and open to remote and relocation.

  • I work end to end. Research, user flows, wireframes, final visuals and developer handoff. No gaps for a team to fill.
  • I have shipped under pressure. Real clients, short timelines, government stakeholders. The work still made it to launch.
  • I design with systems. Tokens and reusable components, so quality holds up as the product grows.
